About us

Roy Ann Diner is one of the best diner restaurants in Bucks County. We pride ourselves on quality food, excellent service, fast friendly service and affordable prices since 1953. Bring the whole family! All of our menus are available for you to browse our vast selections of fresh, homemade soups, salads, entrees and desserts.
